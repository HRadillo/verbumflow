# **App Name**: VerbumFlow

## Core Features:

- Fill-in-the-Blank Input: Allow users to complete verb conjugations in a fill-in-the-blank format, testing their knowledge of specific forms.
- Multiple-Choice Selection: Present verb conjugations as multiple-choice questions, offering an alternative practice method.
- Instant Feedback: Provide immediate feedback on the correctness of the user's input.
- Targeted Practice: Enable users to select specific verbs or tenses to focus their practice sessions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft blue (#A0D2EB) to promote a calm, focused learning environment. It reflects clarity and trust.
- Background color: Off-white (#F8F8FF), very slightly desaturated from the primary color, offers a clean and distraction-free backdrop, suitable for prolonged use.
- Accent color: A slightly darker, subtly distinct blue (#7CB9E8) is used for key interactive elements, such as the submit button and focus states. Its lighter saturation compared to the primary enhances visibility without being too jarring.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif font. Its modern, machined, objective, neutral look is suitable for educational content.
- Use simple, clear icons to represent different verb tenses and functionalities within the app.
- Maintain a clean, organized layout to minimize distractions and improve the user's focus on learning.
- Incorporate subtle transitions and feedback animations to enhance the user experience without being distracting.
